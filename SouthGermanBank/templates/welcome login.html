      Getting started!


     1) To begin, you will need to signup on our website.
     2) After signup, Login via link in the navigation bar.
     3) Then you can choose the application you wish to use (1. Analyser Application ; 2. CRUD Operations Application)
     4) Go to the Analyser Application and fill the form containing the particulars of the concerned customer and Predict .
     5) The Application will redirect you to the page containing the values of the particulars of the customer and the Predicted Credit Risk (High Risk OR Low Risk) using Logistic Regression.
     6) It will also show the data of all the customers in the database.


     a) If you wish to add the details of the customer into the database (in "SGBProject_Odb" model), click on the back button.
     b) Go to the CRUD Operations Application.
     c) Add the details of the customer in the form under "Add new" tab and click on "Add new" .
     d) Remember the customer id if you wish to edit/delete the customer details.
